Ha Young Sun Projects

The Cocoon

First-Year Studio
2025
Seoul National University

PROJECT

The Cocoon is a proposal for a contextual and site-specific interpretation of parasitic architecture. Attached to the wall of the large stair void between the first and second floors of Building 39 at Seoul National University, it provides a small retreat for a single occupant.

The site is overwhelmingly vast yet underutilized, remaining unlit after dark and posing a potential risk at night. The cocoon-like structure attached to the stair railing functions as a private resting space, a lookout that enables passive observation, and, when unoccupied at night, a lantern that illuminates the surrounding space.

A person resting inside naturally becomes an unintentional observer of the space below. The presence of someone—even without the intention to watch—can influence behavior, encouraging people to become more mindful in a shared space than they would in complete solitude.
